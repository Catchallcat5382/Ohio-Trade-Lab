HOW BIDDING WORKS

People bid with in-game skins and items, not cash. The highest-value valid item bid is shown while the auction is active. When time runs out, the highest bidder and owner automatically receive a private room.

The auction stays in Delivery Pending until the owner confirms the auctioned item was delivered. If it is never delivered, the owner may cancel that result and rerun the auction.
